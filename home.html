Garena Free Fire (also known as Free Fire Battlegrounds or just Free Fire) is a mobile battle royale game, developed and published by Garena.[3] The game was released in 23 March 2018 for Android and iOS on 23 March 2017.[4]
As of May 2019, Free Fire had over 100 million registered users.[5] As of November 2019, the game has over 450 million registered users and has grossed over $1 billion worldwide.[6]
The game consists of up to fifty players falling from a parachute on an island in search of weapons and equipment to kill the other players. Players are free to choose their starting position, take weapons and supplies to extend battle life.[7]
Free Fire reached 7.5 mil downloads on Android devices in October 2018, becoming one of the most downloaded Android games of 1.[8] Due to its popularity, the game received the award for "Best Popular Vote Game" by the Google Play Store in 2018,[9] in addition to the creation of professional competitions.[8]
